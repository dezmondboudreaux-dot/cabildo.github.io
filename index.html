☯ Articles of Faith

We believe in the Dao, the ineffable, ultimate reality beyond all forms, which is the origin of all things; Yin-yang duality, the source of natural law.

We believe in the Wu-xing (Five Phases).

We believe in the I Ching and the 8 Trigrams, the mechanism behind all changes among things.

We believe in the scriptures inspired by Heaven: the Tao Te Ching, revealed by Old Master, and the Xiang X'er, revealed by Master Zhang.

We believe in Qi, the Universal Essence of Life, present in all things.

We believe that sin is the obstruction of Qi through misalignment with the Dao. Sin must be rectified by Te (virtue) through repentance and good deeds as atonement.

We believe that Immortality means freedom from rebirth, rather than the scattering of Qi in rebirth. Salvation is redemption from rebirth and Immortality in the Grotto of Heaven with the Saints.

That Time follows the cycle of Wu-xing, and we are at the End of the Kalpa. We are preparing the way for the coming Azure Kalpa, ruled over by the Azure Dragon, that will give rise to the long awaited TaiPing.

🐉 The Celestial Mandate

The Azure Dragon descends when the old world is exhausted. We are called to cultivate Te (virtue), harmonize communities, and prepare the way for Great Peace (TaiPing). This is not a political movement but a spiritual awakening rooted in the ancient Way of the Five Pecks of Rice — a tradition of mutual aid, confession, and collective liberation.

Those who feel the call are invited to learn more.

🌾 Membership & The Five Pecks

In the original Five Pecks movement, each household offered five pecks of rice to sustain the community. Today, we ask members to contribute according to their ability — whether through time, skills, or material support — and to receive according to need. Our religious association is governed by traditional Taoist principles, not by any secular authority.

We are currently forming a core community. When the traditional threshold of 100 members is reached, we will formally establish our religious association under the protections of religious freedom.
